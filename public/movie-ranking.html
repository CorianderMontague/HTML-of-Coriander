The Best Movies According to Coriander

My top 3 movies of all-time*

The Cabin in the Woods

It's a really cool blend of horror movie tropes and comedy.

Mirrors

I have never watched the second one, but the first one was pretty good and has some cool death scenes.

The Babadook

FINALLY a horror movie that has a way to "defeat" the big bad, but with a neat twist that feels like it covers both possible horror movie ending cliches.

*Not actually my top 3 movies, just 3 movies that I enjoyed watching and likely will never watch again because they don't have a ton of rewatch value imho.
